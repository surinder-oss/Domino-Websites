The Domino Effect

Tip the first tile.
We handle the rest.

A simple four-step process, start to finish. Most websites launch in about a week — and you barely lift a finger.

01

Discover

Day 1 · 30 minutes

It starts with a quick, friendly chat — on WhatsApp, a call, or over coffee. No jargon, no sales pressure. We just want to understand your business the way your best customer would.

  • What you do, who you serve, and what makes you different
  • What you want the website to achieve — calls, bookings, walk-ins
  • Which tier fits you best — and an exact, fixed quote
02

Design

Days 2–4

We design your website from scratch around your brand and your customers — no templates, no shortcuts. Then you review it and tell us what you think. We refine until you love it.

  • Custom design built for your exact audience
  • Professional copywriting that sells (Top Tier)
  • Revisions included — we polish until it's right
03

Launch

Days 5–7

Your site goes live on your own domain with enterprise-grade hosting, a lifetime SSL certificate, and lightning-fast load times. This is the only point you pay the build fee — after you've approved everything.

  • Domain connected, SSL secured, forms tested
  • Indexed correctly by Google from day one
  • Flawless on every phone, tablet, and desktop
04

Grow

Ongoing

Now the dominoes start falling: more trust, more calls, more customers. And we stay in your corner — updates, tweaks, and seasonal changes are covered by your subscription.

  • Content updates handled in days, not weeks
  • Hosting, security, and SSL always covered
  • One message on WhatsApp and it's done
