Questions, answered.

What counts as a content update?＋
Changing text, swapping a photo, updating your hours or contact info — the everyday changes a business needs. Most updates are completed within 1–2 business days. Full redesigns or brand-new pages are quoted separately.
How fast will my website launch?＋
Most Basic sites launch in about a week. Top Tier sites typically take 2–3 weeks depending on the number of pages and how quickly we receive your photos and business details.
When am I charged?＋
The build fee is charged once, at launch — after you've seen and approved your website. The annual subscription starts the same day and renews automatically every 365 days. You can cancel the renewal anytime.
What happens if I cancel the subscription?＋
The site is yours — we don't hold it hostage. If you cancel, your site stays up until the end of your paid year, and we'll hand over the files so you can host it anywhere.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Top Tier later?＋
Absolutely. You only pay the difference in build fees, and your subscription adjusts at the next renewal. Many clients start with Basic and upgrade once the new leads start rolling in.
